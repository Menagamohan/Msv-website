Safety first

Chimney sweeping is much more than a formality: it helps prevent the risks of fire and carbon monoxide poisoning. A poorly maintained chimney can become dangerous for your home. That is why our certified chimney sweeps operate with rigor and professionalism.

If you use a wood or pellet stove, insert or fireplace, regular maintenance is essential.

Our services guarantee optimal draft and improved performance of your appliance. This allows you to enjoy the warmth of winter with complete peace of mind.

Services tailored to all types of installations

Our teams are trained to work on all types of flues, whether traditional or metal. We also clear nests or deposits that could obstruct the chimney. Each intervention is carried out with appropriate equipment, for a thorough cleaning without damaging your installations. We comply with current standards and provide you with a compliant chimney sweeping certificate, which is essential for your home insurance.

Plan ahead for a worry-free winter

To avoid the winter rush, consider scheduling your chimney sweeping in the fall. This allows you to start the heating season stress-free, with a clean and ready-to-use system. Our time slots are flexible to fit your schedule. By intervening early, we can also identify potential anomalies before they cause problems. Regular maintenance prolongs the life of your equipment and optimizes its performance.
